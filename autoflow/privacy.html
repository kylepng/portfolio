Auto Flow Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Auto Flow ("we", "our", "us") handles data when you use the Auto Flow Chrome extension.

1. What Auto Flow Does

Auto Flow helps users automate media generation workflows on Google Flow pages and manage downloads and queue operations.

2. Data We Process

Depending on features you use, Auto Flow may process:

  • Account data: email address and authentication/session tokens when you sign in.
  • License/subscription data: plan tier and license status used to enable feature access.
  • User configuration data: extension settings, queue state, mode selections, and preferences stored in browser extension storage.
  • Workflow data: prompts, task metadata, project identifiers/URLs, and media URLs needed for automation and download handling.
  • Local file references: reference images and generated file naming/download metadata needed to execute your selected workflow.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide core automation features (queueing, mode switching, task orchestration).
  • Authenticate users and verify subscription/license status.
  • Save your extension preferences and session state.
  • Trigger and manage browser downloads you request.
  • Diagnose reliability issues when logs/debug features are used.

4. Where Data Is Stored

  • Local/browser storage: most settings, queue state, and runtime data are stored on your device in extension storage/IndexedDB.
  • Cloud services: account, auth, and license/config requests may be processed through our backend services (including Supabase-hosted endpoints).

5. Third-Party Services

Auto Flow interacts with third-party services required for functionality, including:

  • Google Flow pages that you choose to automate.
  • Supabase infrastructure used for authentication/license/config operations.
  • Payment/subscription processors when applicable to your purchase flow.

Third-party services process data under their own privacy policies.

6. Permissions Use (Chrome Extension)

  • activeTab, tabs, scripting, sidePanel: to run automation UI/actions on supported pages.
  • storage: to save user settings, queue state, and runtime metadata.
  • downloads: to create and manage files you explicitly request to download.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as needed to provide the service (for example, authentication/license checks, infrastructure providers, or legal compliance).

8. Data Retention

  • Local extension data remains on your device until you clear extension data or uninstall the extension.
  • Account/license records may be retained for operational, billing, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes.

9. Your Choices

  • Do not sign in if you do not want account-linked features.
  • Clear stored extension data and browser downloads at any time.
  • Uninstall Auto Flow to stop all extension data processing.

10. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards for service operations, but no internet or storage system is 100% secure.

11. Children

Auto Flow is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

13. Contact
